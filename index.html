About Gems Bonanza

Gems Bonanza is a vivid Pragmatic Play slot released in October 2020 and built around an unconventional 8x8 layout.

Instead of classic paylines, it uses Cluster Pays, where wins come from 5 or more matching symbols connected horizontally or vertically. Every winning cluster triggers a Tumble, so one good hit can grow into a longer chain of cascading payouts. The gameplay becomes more exciting through colored marker features that activate Nuclear, Wild Gem, Squares, Colossal Symbol, and Lucky Wilds.

Its signature bonus is Gold Fever, where all modifiers are guaranteed in sequence and the multiplier can climb from x2 to x10. Overall, this is a high-volatility game designed for players who enjoy chasing bigger bursts rather than constant small returns.
